Work Background

As a web developer for a small NCMHCE (National Clinical Mental Health Counseling Examination) exam prep company in Florida, I focus on creating engaging educational tools to help aspiring mental health counselors succeed. My role involves developing interactive quiz games that reinforce key concepts from the exam, making learning both fun and effective. I work closely with a small team of content creators and mental health professionals to ensure the games are educationally sound and user-friendly. By blending my technical skills with a passion for education, I help make exam prep more accessible and enjoyable for students pursuing their counseling careers.
